## Overview

This repository contains the complete work, research, documentation, presentations, and reports completed during the Raritone Summer Internship 2026.

The internship focused on exploring Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision, Virtual Try-On Systems, Fashion Recommendation Systems, and AR/VR technologies for the fashion industry.

Throughout the internship, various technologies, models, datasets, workflows, and fashion AI applications were researched and documented to understand how intelligent systems can improve online shopping experiences.

---

## Internship Objectives

The primary objectives of the internship were:

- Study Human Body Detection techniques.
- Explore Human Pose Estimation systems.
- Understand Body Measurement Extraction methods.
- Research Cloth Segmentation and Human Parsing.
- Analyze 2D Virtual Try-On technologies.
- Explore 3D Virtual Try-On and AR/VR systems.
- Study AI Fashion Recommendation Systems.
- Research Fashion Datasets.
- Design complete AI workflows for fashion applications.
- Document findings and future improvement opportunities.
